The Story

Born from the streets,
built on raw words.

Toxic Lyrikali is a Nairobi-based artist whose music sits at the crossroads of Hip-Hop, Afro-Trap and raw spoken word. Growing up in the heart of Kenya's capital, he found his voice in the rhythm of the streets.

"Music is the only language that needs no translation — I just speak what I feel, and let the beat carry it home."

— Toxic Lyrikali
