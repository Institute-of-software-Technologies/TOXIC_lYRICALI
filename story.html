When the night becomes the beat

It started in the backroom of a crowded Nairobi studio, where the air smelled like espresso and restless energy. Toxic sat cross-legged on the floor, scribbling bars on the corner of a torn poster while the speakers rattled the walls. The city outside felt loud, but inside, all that mattered was the next line, the next story that had to be told.

The world didn’t know the names yet, but the rhythm was already taking shape—raw, unruly, and honest. One voice rose above the noise, ready to spark a new generation. That was the moment when a story became a movement.

Now, every pitch, every verse, and every track is an invitation: bring your truth, bring your fire, and let it stand on the page. This is where the story meets the beat.
